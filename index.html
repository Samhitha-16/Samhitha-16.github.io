OVERVIEW

An Integrated facility for Machining and Pressure Die casting, driven by engineering professionals who are passionate about metal cutting innovatively, creatively, and profitably. We manufacture and supply many critical components made out of ferrous and non-ferrous for automotive and non-automotive sectors supplied to domestic and international customers. The founders are from leading machine tool industries in India and believe in giving the best value to the customers. This is accomplished by adopting the most productive machining methods, strategic initiatives of Backward integrations to pressure die casting and forward integration to assembly's, supported by robust infrastructure, assisted by international supply chain, certified by international certifications, driven by creative leadership.

We deliver value to our customers, not just components.

Our Strength

Success Lies in Bringing Ideas to Life

Precision is not just for the components. We carryout the entire process of new product development and subsequent Series of production with precise planning, process and method and there by achieving ultimate quality , cost and delivery performace to the customer.

It was a strategic move to backward integrate our activity to solve the quality issues in high pressure die casting processes . This eventually turnedout to be a great strength in our offering to the customers by reducing the waste in our processes

Our quality and offerings are well received in international markets. We export to 7 European and American countries. More than 52% of our revenue is from International business. We are recognized by DGFT as star status holder for our performance in foreign trade

Our protoshop makes our machining experience very unique and valuable as we make one of components needed for R&D Labs, Universities , New developments etc. Our dedicated machining and measuring facility can take any challenging component and we can even make 1 pc per batch !

Our supply chain is a well matured set up. We source raw material from several leading Aluminium, Steel, Stainless Steel, Seamless Tube manufacturers across the globe. WE buy directly from the mill so that we are the competitive.

We are associated with a strong and very knowledgeable vendor base for secondary operations like Heat Treatment, Zinc Plating, Copper Plating , Anodizing, Beed Blasting, Chrome Plating , Blackening, Industrial painting and so on

Distinct about distinct is that we build our own Dies, Own Fixtures , Own Frugal Automations, Own Robotic Automations and finally our own CNC Special Purpose Machines. Due to this capability we can make our thoughts in to reality much faster than others in the industry.

Our factory is a smart factory. Out in house developed ioT model “My Machine” is able to monitor production , rejection, Idle time , operator performance etc through a ioT gateway. It helps in maintaining our competitive edge and still be competitive

Frequently Asked Questions

What size of die casting component you can handle ?

We can handle up to 1.6 Kg weight with or without cores. However for bigger components we work with our strategic partners who are capable to handle bigger size components.

What size of Machined components you can handle ?

Our VMCs are with 1 Meter bed and turning s are with Dia 165 chuck. Primarily we are specialized in small size components.

What kind of materials are machined in DPS ?

• WE are experts in any metal cutting. However 70 % of the material we machine are Aluminium extrusions of
6068 T6, 6061 T66 , Die casting materails like ADC 12 / LM 4 and so on.

• WE also machine steels such as SAE 1018, SAE 1045, EN1A , EN19C, 16MnCr5, 20 Mn Cr5.

• SS 316, SS303, SS304 are SS grades which are generally machined in our machines.

• In the Proto Shop, we also machine exotic materials like Titanium and other non metals like Rubber, Plastic, Delrin, ABB, PEEK, POM A/B etc.

• Except Cast Iron, we are machining all materials.

What are the Make of machines in DPS ?

• WE are with more than 100 CNC Machines. Most of our machiens are from Ace / AMS. We also use some machines of BFW , Battery of Fanuc Robo Drills, Kira , Marshall etc. We use make in India machines to achieve accuracies and productivity.

• We have technocrat make 180 T High Pressure Die casting machines.

• We believe that the best cost per pc is possible to achieve using Indian machines only. Quality is qualifier and we are achieving quality using these machines.

What are the Type of machines in DPS ?

• We have CNC Turning, CNC Twin Spindle Turning, CNC Polygon Turning, CNC 4 Spindle Turning Machines. WE have 3 Axis, 3.5 Axis and 4 Axis CNC Vertical Machining Centers with and without pallet Changers.

• Few of our machines are equipped with Robot integration for loading / unloading.

Why do you call yourselves as “DISTINCT”?

We are distinct because, unlike other component manufacturers, we dont just deliver components. WE ensure that we add value to you the customers business by giving more than what we get by virtue of our own Dies, Fixtures, Automations and Machines.

Which quality certifications do you have ?

• Our systems and procedures are certified by TUV Nord for ISO9001: 2015 standards. We are also certified by TUV Nord for Environment management systems ISO 14001 : 2015 standards.

• Also our systems and procedures are qualified for the normative references of VDA 6.3 standards which are required to export to the European brands of automotives.

Why you are still not qualified for IATF16949:2016 quality certifications Yet ?

Yes, we are not IATF 16949: 2016 as we were not catering our components to any major automotive OEMS hence we had not ventured into IATF. However in light of our entry to a leading 3 wheeler EV, we have now initiated IATF certification with TUV Nord. WE are expected to have IATF by end of 2023.

Do you use any Russian origin material ?

No, We use all Indian origin materials for all our products. We also certify all our materials with ROHS compliance certificate for its substances.
