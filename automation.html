automation

Objective of using Automation in DPS is to Reduce the effort of Operator and increase the manpower productivity

In manufacturing context, the objective of Automation is not to eliminate the operator. The automation is required to minimize the efforts of the operator so that they can concentrate on multi machine operating, measurements , 5S near machines etc so that the overall effectiveness of the whole machining systems in improved considerably. It also helps to transfer the operator skill to the machining system. Keeping the importance of automation in manufacturing, DPS developed in-house competency in Mechatronics to develop frugal automation projects.

• Robot Integrations to carryout repetitive jobs
• Robot integration in difficult to work areas, Die casting
• PLC Controlled small automations
• Safety curtains for the presses
• Digital Poke Yokes
• Air Savers in air gauges
