ABOUT US

Distinct Productivity Solutions is a Bangalore Based, export oriented precision component manufacturer. The founders are from strong technical background of manufacturing technology. Passionate about cutting the metal creatively and innovatively we manufacture competitive products with uncompromised quality and deliver to domestic and international customer base

45%of our produce are exported to many European countries, USA and Canada. Distinct about us is that we retain competitive edge by in-house developed capabilities like development fixtures, dies, moulds, special purpose machines, frugal automations, Industry 4.0 solutions. With this in-house capability, the ideas are transformed into reality much faster to reap the benefits. We are a SME with 3 manufacturing locations, International Certifications, fleet of 100+ CNC machines and 200+ skilled and motivated team.

History & Heritage

Company History

Commenced in 2004 as a very humble beginning after 3 decades of machine tool application experience. Started with cutting tool sales. Right from day one, we adopted solution provider approach. We tried to give solutions to manufacturing problems through tools coupled with our manufacturing knowledge.It was well accepted by the customers and we started seeing many opportunities to design, development and supply of fixtures, precision component manufacturing etc. With the help of one of our esteemed customer , we started our component manufacturing activity. By virtue of contentious hardwork and creative leadership, we are one of the prominent players in the precision component manufacturing domain. Here is the glimpse of our growth story

  1. 2004 Commenced operations with Trading SECO Cutting tools and precision component manufacturing
  2. 2005 Commencing of Design and Development of fixtures as a tool of Productivity improvement.
  3. 2007 Certified ISO 9001:2004
  4. 2008Commencement of Precision component Manufacturing.
  5. 2010 First export shipment to USA and Canada
  6. 2013 Entry to Bosch Rexroth to import substitute a critical component
  7. 2014 Awarded best supplier by “Bosch Rexroth” for outstanding performance.
  8. 2015 Multi year Export contract to Italy : Bunch of 43 components required for forklifts.
  9. 2016 Moved to own unit in Kumbalagodu, Mysore Road, Bangalore
  10. 2016 ISO 14001:2015 certification for environment management
  11. 2017 Backward integration to Aluminium High Pressure Die casting
  12. 2017 Qualification for VDA 6.3 certification P1 & P2 audits
  13. 2018Association with VISTHAR in USA for marketing initiatives
  14. 2018 Started exports to Bosch Rexroth AG, Germany, Gear Pump division
  15. 2018 Became a part of Sona Group of Industries , a strategic initiative for growth and expansion
  16. 2018 Won SME Productivity Championship award for productivity case study in National Productivity Summit held in Grand Chola, Chennai.
  17. 2019 Commecing production in unit - 2 , Hosur.
  18. 2020 Smart factory initiatives to develop IoT model for machine monitoring.
  19. 2022 Backward integration to produce aluminium gravity die casting in strategic partnership.
