Industry 4.0

Objective of Industry 4.0 capability in DPS is to Make our machines SMART !

Manufacturing is in the most exciting times of digital era. Many problems that were not possible to solve by conventional methods are being solved by digital methods. DPS sensed the importance of this and researched to result in a product by name MyMachine. The factory is completely paperless and we do get many analytical tips for improving the overall equipment effectiveness ( OEE ) , Operator efficiency, top 3 rejections , top3 idletimes etc. There are lot of benefits that we can reap by implementing Industry 4.0 solutions

• Personal Gadget of the operator
• Operator Knows his own performance
• Increases operator involvement in the work
• Many aspects become paperless reporting
• About 15% improvement in productivity
